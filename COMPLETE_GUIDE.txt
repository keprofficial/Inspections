🔥 COMMON ERRORS & FIXES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ERROR: "flutter : The term 'flutter' is not recognized..."

  CAUSE: Flutter not installed or not in PATH

  FIXES:
  1. Verify Flutter is extracted to C:\flutter
  2. Verify C:\flutter\bin is in System PATH
  3. Close ALL PowerShell/CMD windows
  4. Restart your computer
  5. Open NEW PowerShell window
  6. Try: flutter --version

  See: INSTALL_FLUTTER.md for detailed help


ERROR: "No connected devices"

  CAUSE: Device/emulator not available

  FIXES:
  1. Open Android Emulator (Android Studio)
     OR
     Connect physical phone via USB
  2. Run: flutter devices
  3. Should show your device


ERROR: First build takes forever

  NORMAL! This is expected behavior.
  • First build: 5-10 minutes
  • Subsequent builds: 1-2 minutes
  • Keep internet connection active
